# External Links & Further Reading

Explore the practical frameworks for safely incorporating profound cyberdelic experiences into ongoing therapy, coaching, or personal growth practices.

### Scientific Papers & Frameworks
- [The Importance of Integration in Psychedelic Treatment (Frontiers in Psychiatry)](https://www.frontiersin.org/articles/10.3389/fpsyt.2022.824077/full) - While substance-focused, the framework of mapping the altered state to daily behavioral change maps perfectly to cyberdelic integration.
- [Set and Setting in Cyberdelic Interventions](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/Set_and_Setting_in_Digital_Therapies) - A conceptual framework applying Leary's classic psychedelic rules to the deployment of virtual health.

### Articles & Best Practices
- [Why Integration is the Most Important Part of Your Trip](https://psychedelicstoday.com/2019/07/26/integration-most-important-part-of-trip/) - Discusses why the experience itself is only the catalyst, and the real "work" happens in the weeks following.
- [Digital Ethics and the Responsibility of the VR Creator](https://skarredghost.com/2021/04/09/ethics-virtual-reality/) - An important discussion on the ethical duty of care facilitators have when exposing clients to intense ontological shifts.

### Books & Guides
- [Psychedelic Integration: Psychotherapy for Non-Ordinary States of Consciousness by Marc Aixala](https://www.amazon.com/Psychedelic-Integration-Psychotherapy-Non-Ordinary-Consciousness/dp/0907791882) - The definitive manual for therapists helping clients process profound non-ordinary states.
- [The Psychedelic Explorer's Guide by James Fadiman](https://www.amazon.com/Psychedelic-Explorers-Guide-Therapeutic-Journeys/dp/1594774021) - Offers practical guidance on prepping spaces ("setting") and intentions ("set") which apply heavily to cyberdelic session design.

### Protocols and Networks
- [Fireside Project](https://firesideproject.org/) - A peer support line demonstrating community models for integration and harm reduction following altered states.
- [ZENDO Project](https://zendoproject.org/) - MAPS' psychedelic harm reduction organization, whose core tenets (create a safe space, sitting not guiding, talking through not down) are the bedrock of cyberdelic facilitation.
