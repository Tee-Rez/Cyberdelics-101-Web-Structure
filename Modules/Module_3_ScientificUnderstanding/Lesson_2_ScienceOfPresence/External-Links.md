# External Links & Further Reading

Dive into the mechanics of spatial computing, embodiment, and the psychological concept of "presence" that powers immersive cyberdelics.

### Scientific Papers & Research
- [Place Illusion and Plausibility can lead to Realistic Behaviour in Immersive Virtual Environments](https://royalsocietypublishing.org/doi/10.1098/rstb.2009.0138) - Mel Slater's seminal framework defining the two pillars of virtual presence (PI and PSI).
- [The Rubber Hand Illusion and Virtual Embodiment](https://www.nature.com/articles/391756a0) - The foundational study demonstrating how easily the brain can map ownership onto a non-biological limb or avatar.

### Articles & Essays
- [What is "Presence" in VR?](https://www.roadtovr.com/understanding-presence-in-virtual-reality/) - An industry breakdown of the neurological thresholds required to convince the brain a digital world is real.
- [The Neuroscience of Virtual Reality (Frontiers in Neuroscience)](https://www.frontiersin.org/articles/10.3389/fncom.2017.00030/full) - Explores how VR hacks the sensorimotor loop to create profound illusions of space.

### Books & Literature
- [Experience on Demand by Jeremy Bailenson](https://www.amazon.com/Experience-Demand-Virtual-Reality-Works/dp/0393253694) - Chronicles extensive lab research on how VR embodiment fundamentally shifts behavioral psychology.
- [The Infinite Retina by Irena Cronin and Robert Scoble](https://www.amazon.com/Infinite-Retina-Spatial-Computing-Business/dp/1838827568) - Discusses the convergence of spatial computing hardware that generates deep presence.

### Talks & Presentations
- [Mel Slater on Virtual Reality and the Brain (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=13Q1v70kRRs) - A lecture by the leading academic figure on VR presence and body ownership illusions.
