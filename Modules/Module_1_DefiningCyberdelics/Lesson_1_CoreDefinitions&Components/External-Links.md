# External Links & Further Reading

These resources dive deeper into the core definitions and fundamental components of cyberdelic technologies.

### Scientific Papers & Research
- [A Review of Virtual Reality as a Medium for Altered States of Consciousness](https://www.frontiersin.org/articles/10.3389/fpsyg.2019.02667/full) - Explores how VR can induce states similar to those brought on by psychedelics.
- [The therapeutic potential of biofeedback and neurofeedback](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC4892319/) - A deep dive into the physiological components often integrated into interactive cyberdelic systems.

### Articles & Essays
- [What are Cyberdelics?](https://www.vice.com/en/article/yp3p37/what-are-cyberdelics-virtual-reality-psychedelics) - A great introductory Vice article on the intersection of technology and visionary experiences.
- [The Rise of Techno-Spiritualism and Cyberdelics](https://psychedelicstoday.com/2020/06/02/the-rise-of-techno-spiritualism-and-cyberdelics/) - Discusses the cultural shift towards using tech for spiritual growth rather than mere entertainment.

### Books & Literature
- [TechGnosis: Myth, Magic, and Mysticism in the Age of Information by Erik Davis](https://www.erikdavis.org/books/techgnosis) - Examines the mystical impulses behind modern technology and computation.
