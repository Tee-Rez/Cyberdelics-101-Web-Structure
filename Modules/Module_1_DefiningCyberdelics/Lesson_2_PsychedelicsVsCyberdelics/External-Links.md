# External Links & Further Reading

These resources explore the comparative phenomenology and overlapping experiences between chemical psychedelics and cyberdelic technologies.

### Scientific Papers & Research
- [Isness-D: A VR Experience that Mimics the Overview Effect](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41598-022-19242-y) - A Nature Scientific Reports paper demonstrating how VR can match the intensity of medium-dose psychedelic experiences.
- [Comparing the Phenomenology of Virtual Reality and Classic Psychedelics](https://royalsocietypublishing.org/doi/10.1098/rstb.2021.0084) - Examines the subjective overlap between immersive VR and substance-induced states of mind.

### Articles & Essays
- [Virtual Reality and Psychedelic Simulations for Treating Psychological Disorders](https://www.mdpi.com/2673-4060/5/2/31) - A comprehensive 2024 review of the therapeutic parallels between VR and psychedelics.
- [Immersive Media and Altered States of Consciousness](https://www.frontiersin.org/articles/10.3389/frvir.2023.1116518/full) - Discusses the ways sound and light technology seek to trigger endogenous psychedelic responses.

### Books & Literature
- [How to Change Your Mind by Michael Pollan](https://michaelpollan.com/books/how-to-change-your-mind/) - Foundational text setting the baseline for the neuroscience of altered states.

### Projects & Studies
- [Virtual Reality as a Psychedelic Simulation for Mental Health](https://maps.org/news/bulletin/virtual-reality-as-a-psychedelic-simulation-for-mental-health/) - A MAPS Bulletin exploration of consciousness expansion and therapy via VR.
