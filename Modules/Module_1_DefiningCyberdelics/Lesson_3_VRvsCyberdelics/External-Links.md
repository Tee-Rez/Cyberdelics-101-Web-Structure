# External Links & Further Reading

Explore the distinction between conventional virtual reality applications and transcendent cyberdelic VR experiences.

### Scientific Papers & Research
- [A Sense of Presence in Virtual Reality](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC6410491/) - A review of what constitutes "presence" in VR, a foundational concept for differentiating standard VR from cyberdelic VR.
- [Embodiment and the Sense of Self in Virtual Reality](https://www.frontiersin.org/articles/10.3389/frobt.2016.00049/full) - Explores how avatars and immersive environments actively alter our fundamental sense of self and body representation.

### Articles & Essays
- [Beyond Gaming: When VR Becomes a Tool for Transformation](https://www.roadtovr.com/beyond-gaming-vr-transformation-meditation/) - Discusses the shift from entertainment VR to experiential, transformative systems in modern software.
- [The Philosophy of Virtual Reality](https://aeon.co/essays/how-virtual-reality-is-changing-our-understanding-of-the-world) - An Aeon essay analyzing the ontological implications and emotional impact of immersive digital environments.

### Books & Literature
- [Dawn of the New Everything by Jaron Lanier](https://www.amazon.com/Dawn-New-Everything-Encounters-Reality/dp/1627794093) - A history of VR by one of its pioneers, touching on its early psychedelic and counter-cultural inspirations.

### Projects & Applications
- [ResearchVR Podcast](https://researchvr.podbean.com/) - Deep dives into the cognitive science behind virtual reality and perception.
