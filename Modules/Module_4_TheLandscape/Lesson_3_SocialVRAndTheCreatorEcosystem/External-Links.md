# External Links & Further Reading

Discover the user-generated side of the cyberdelic landscape, where massive online platforms enable creators to construct shared, transcendent spaces.

### Social Platforms
- [VRChat](https://hello.vrchat.com/) - The preeminent social VR platform, home to thousands of user-created psychedelic and ambient worlds.
- [Resonite (formerly Neos VR)](https://resonite.com/) - A highly versatile spatial computing platform known for deep customizability and collaborative world-building.

### Articles & Creator Culture
- [The Club Kids of VRChat](https://www.polygon.com/2021/11/17/22786311/vrchat-dance-clubs-dj-music-rave-culture) - Explores the underground music and rave scene happening nightly inside social VR.
- [Finding Peace in VRChat’s Most Beautiful Worlds](https://mixed-news.com/en/vrchat-the-most-beautiful-worlds-to-relax-in/) - Highlights the specific "chill" or ambient worlds designed for meditation and sensory exploration.

### Notable Creators & Projects
- [The VR Museum of Fine Art](https://store.steampowered.com/app/515020/The_VR_Museum_of_Fine_Art/) - An early example of high-fidelity, self-paced cultural exploration.
- [Goliath: Playing With Reality](https://goliathvr.io/) - A phenomenal VR narrative exploring schizophrenia, gaming, and social connection.

### Documentaries & Essays
- [We Met in Virtual Reality (HBO Documentary)](https://www.hbo.com/movies/we-met-in-virtual-reality) - A groundbreaking documentary filmed entirely inside VRChat, showcasing the depth of social presence and avatar embodiment.
- [The Philosophy of Avatars](https://www.wired.com/story/vrchat-avatars-identity/) - A look at how choosing our own digital representation affects psychology and self-perception.
