# External Links & Further Reading

Resources focusing on the clinical implementations of immersive media and biofeedback hardware in medical, psychological, and wellness settings.

### Clinical Platforms & Solutions
- [AppliedVR](https://appliedvr.io/) - A pioneer in therapeutic virtual reality, specifically focused on pain management and behavioral modification.
- [Amelia Virtual Care (formerly Psious)](https://ameliavirtualcare.com/) - A leading platform for VR exposure therapy utilized by mental health professionals.
- [HeartMath Institute](https://www.heartmath.org/) - Leaders in biofeedback, specifically focusing on Heart Rate Variability (HRV) and emotional self-regulation.

### Scientific Papers & Research
- [The Clinical Utility of Virtual Reality Experiences (APA)](https://www.apa.org/monitor/2019/10/cover-virtual-reality) - The American Psychological Association's overview of VR as a clinical tool.

### Articles & Case Studies
- [Using Biofeedback to Train the Brain](https://www.nytimes.com/2018/06/07/well/mind/biofeedback-meditation-stress-anxiety.html) - Mainstream coverage of how monitoring physiological signals allows for greater agency over autonomic responses.
- [FDA Approves First VR Treatment for Chronic Back Pain](https://www.fda.gov/news-events/press-announcements/fda-authorizes-marketing-virtual-reality-system-chronic-pain-reduction) - The official FDA authorization for AppliedVR's RelieVRx system.

### Devices & Hardware
- [Muse Headband (Interaxon)](https://choosemuse.com/) - A consumer EEG device that gamifies neurofeedback for meditation.
