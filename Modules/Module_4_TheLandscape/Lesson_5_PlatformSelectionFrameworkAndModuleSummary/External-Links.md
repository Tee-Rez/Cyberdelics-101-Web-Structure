# External Links & Further Reading

Resources dedicated to evaluating appropriate hardware and software systems depending on your specific goals (clinical, consumer, or creative).

### Hardware Evaluation & Consumer Reviews
- [Road to VR: Hardware Guide](https://www.roadtovr.com/category/hardware/) - Continually updated reviews and specs for the latest immersive hardware.
- [UploadVR: Best Alternatives to Meta Quest](https://uploadvr.com/best-vr-headsets/) - A vital guide for those looking at standalone and PCVR ecosystems outside the dominant Meta ecosystem.

### Ethical & Privacy Considerations
- [Extended Reality and Privacy (EFF)](https://www.eff.org/issues/virtual-reality) - The Electronic Frontier Foundation's deep dive into the privacy implications of deploying biometric-tracking hardware.

### Frameworks & Decision making
- [How to Choose the Right VR Headset for Enterprise and Therapy](https://virtualrealitypop.com/) - Articles focusing on the friction points (setup time, hygiene, battery life) of utilizing cyberdelics in professional settings.
