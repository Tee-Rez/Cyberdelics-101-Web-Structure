# External Links & Further Reading

Explore the leading commercially available applications specifically designed from the ground-up to facilitate cyberdelic and transformative states.

### Purpose-Built Cyberdelic Platforms
- [TRIPP VR](https://www.tripp.com/) - Perhaps the most well-known consumer cyberdelic platform, combining breathing exercises, kaleidoscopic visuals, and guided meditations.
- [SoundSelf: A Technodelic](https://soundself.com/) - A seminal voice-driven VR experience that uses the user's own chanting to drive symmetric, hypnagogic visuals and induce a trance state.
- [Lumenate: The Altered State App](https://lumenate.com/) - Uses a smartphone flashlight to create stroboscopic effects (Ganzfeld) with closed eyes, democratizing access to non-ordinary states.

### Articles & Platform Reviews
- [Can a VR App Teach You to Meditate? (Wired)](https://www.wired.com/story/can-a-vr-app-teach-you-to-meditate/) - A review of TRIPP and the growing sector of "digital psychedelics."
- [The Making of SoundSelf (Game Developer)](https://www.gamedeveloper.com/design/the-making-of-soundself-a-technodelic) - A deep dive into the design philosophy behind a voice-controlled cyberdelic experience.

### Media & Documentaries
- [Robin Arnott on SoundSelf, TRIPP, and the Future of Digital Therapeutics](https://voicesofvr.com/1000-soundself-vr-as-a-digital-therapeutic-for-psychedelic-therapy/) - A Voices of VR podcast interview outlining the transition of cyberdelics from indie experiments to clinical tools.
- [Isness-D: A VR Experience that Mimics the Overview Effect](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41598-022-19242-y) - Examining the platform specifically designed to replicate the awe reported by astronauts.
