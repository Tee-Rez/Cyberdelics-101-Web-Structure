# External Links & Further Reading

Explore the specialized hardware outside standard VR headsets, including advanced biosensors, haptics, and sensory isolation systems.

### Hardware & Brain-Computer Interfaces (BCI)
- [Galea (by OpenBCI)](https://galea.co/) - A hardware and software platform integrating EEG, EMG, EDA, PPG, and eye-tracking into a single VR/AR headset faceplate.
- [NextMind (now part of Snap)](https://www.roadtovr.com/snap-acquires-nextmind-ar-spectacles-bci/) - Discusses non-invasive neural interfaces that allow users to control digital environments with visual attention.

### Haptics & Somatic Technology
- [SubPac](https://subpac.com/) - A wearable tactile audio system that translates low frequencies directly into the body, crucial for somatic cyberdelic immersion.
- [bHaptics](https://www.bhaptics.com/) - A consumer line of haptic vests and accessories that increase physical embodiment in virtual spaces.

### Sensory Environments
- [The History and Science of Sensory Deprivation Tanks](https://time.com/4106206/sensory-deprivation-tanks-history/) - An overview of float therapy, the low-tech precursor to high-tech cyberdelics.
- [Lucia N°03 Experiential Light Box](https://lucialightexperience.com/) - An example of specialized hypnagogic hardware designed solely to induce trance states via closed-eye light stimulation.

### Books & Forecasting
- [The Fourth Transformation by Robert Scoble & Shel Israel](https://www.amazon.com/Fourth-Transformation-Artificial-Intelligence-Everything/dp/1539413158) - Explores how AR, VR, and BCI hardware will fundamentally change human-computer interaction.
