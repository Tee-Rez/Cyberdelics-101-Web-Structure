# External Links & Further Reading

Investigate the modern convergence of neuro-technology, immersive media, and consciousness research, validating the cyberdelic hypothesis.

### Scientific Papers & Research
- [Psychedelics and Virtual Reality: Parallels and Applications](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/33948011/) - Reviews clinical literature proposing combining the two modalities for enhanced therapeutic outcomes.

### Articles & Deep Dives
- [The Future of Therapy: Mixing Psychedelics with Virtual Reality](https://www.vice.com/en/article/epznyw/mixing-psychedelics-with-virtual-reality) - Discusses "Set and Setting 2.0," utilizing VR to prep and integrate psychedelic sessions.

### Books & Emerging Literature
- [The Neurobiology of the Gods by Erik D. Goodwyn](https://www.amazon.com/Neurobiology-Gods-Evolutionary-Psychology-Religion/dp/0415668988) - Explores how archetypal, profound experiences are hardwired into our biology, which cyberdelics aim to activate.
- [Stealing Fire by Steven Kotler & Jamie Wheal](https://www.stealingfirebook.com/) - Documents the silicon valley executives, Navy SEALs, and researchers currently converging around the optimization of altered states.

### Modern Platforms & Initiatives
- [AWE (Augmented World Expo)](https://www.awexr.com/) - The converging hub for XR professionals, frequently addressing the transformative potential of immersive tech.
- [TransTech Conference](https://www.ttconf.org/) - A conference dedicated entirely to "Transformative Technology" that supports mental wellness, emotional wellbeing, and human flourishing.
