# External Links & Further Reading

Understand the financial engines, venture capital, and governmental regulations shaping what cyberdelic products reach the public.

### Economics & Investment
- [Psychedelic Finance](https://psychedelicfinance.com/) - Tracks the capital flowing into alternative mental health treatments, a parallel indicator for cyberdelic investment.
- [The Economics of the Metaverse (McKinsey Report)](https://www.mckinsey.com/capabilities/growth-marketing-and-sales/our-insights/value-creation-in-the-metaverse) - High-level analysis of the multi-trillion dollar projections driving VR/AR venture funding.
- [ATAI Life Sciences](https://www.atai.life/) - A massive clinical-stage biopharmaceutical company that occasionally invests in digital therapeutics (often accompanying pharmacological trials).

### Regulatory Landscape
- [FDA Digital Health Center of Excellence](https://www.fda.gov/medical-devices/digital-health-center-excellence) - The regulatory body defining what separates a "wellness app" from a "medical device."
- [FDA Breakthrough Devices Program](https://www.fda.gov/medical-devices/how-study-and-market-your-device/breakthrough-devices-program) - The fast-track regulatory process that companies like AppliedVR have successfully navigated.

### Digital Health Policy
- [Digital Therapeutics Alliance (DTA)](https://dtxalliance.org/) - An industry group working to expand access and insurance reimbursement for evidence-based digital treatments.
- [HIPAA and Digital Health (HHS)](https://www.hhs.gov/hipaa/index.html) - Foundational regulations that any clinical cyberdelic application must navigate to protect patient data.
