# External Links & Further Reading

Look at the universities, think tanks, and training programs generating the empirical data and skilled practitioners needed for this field.

### Academic Research Centers
- [Johns Hopkins Center for Psychedelic & Consciousness Research](https://hopkinspsychedelic.org/) - A leading institution churning out gold-standard data on altered states, heavily influencing cyberdelic metrics.
- [Stanford Virtual Human Interaction Lab (VHIL)](https://vhil.stanford.edu/) - Pioneering the psychological understanding of how VR alters behavior, empathy, and perception.

### Think Tanks & Policy Organizations
- [XR Safety Initiative (XRSI)](https://xrsi.org/) - A crucial non-profit establishing standards, privacy guidelines, and ethical frameworks for the spatial computing era.

### Facilitation & Human Infrastructure
- [Fluence: Psychedelic Integration Training](https://www.fluencetraining.com/) - While focused on psychedelics, their comprehensive training for therapists is the current template for "trip sitting" profound digital experiences.
- [Cyberpsychology: Journal of Psychosocial Research on Cyberspace](https://cyberpsychology.eu/) - An academic journal dedicated to understanding the human element in digital health intersections.
