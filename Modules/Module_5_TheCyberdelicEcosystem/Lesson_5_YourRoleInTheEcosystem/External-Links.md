# External Links & Further Reading

Resources for aspiring creators, facilitators, researchers, and psychonauts looking to actively build or practice within the cyberdelic space.

### Career Transitions & Communities
- [XR Bootcamp](https://xrbootcamp.com/) - An excellent starting point for those looking to learn Unity/Unreal specifically for VR/AR development.
- [The Cyberdelic Society Discord / Community](https://cyberdelicsociety.com/) - The best place to network with practicing cyberdelic artists, researchers, and enthusiasts.
- [Voices of VR Podcast (Community Section)](https://voicesofvr.com/) - Listening to the interviews of creators is the best way to understand the varied pathways into the industry.

### Building Your Own Practice
- [How to Build a VR Ready PC](https://www.tomshardware.com/best-picks/best-vr-ready-pcs) - A practical guide for the hardware required to experience high-end, uncompressed cyberdelic art.
- [A Beginner's Guide to Biofeedback](https://www.mindful.org/a-beginners-guide-to-biofeedback/) - Practical steps to starting personal physiological monitoring without clinical-grade equipment.

### Final Inspiration
- [The Overview Effect (Planetary Collective)](https://vimeo.com/55073825) - A beautiful short film summarizing the profound cognitive shift that cyberdelics ultimately aim to recreate and democratize.
