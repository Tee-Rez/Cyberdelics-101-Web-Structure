# External Links & Further Reading

Explore the foundational hardware and software companies building the infrastructure of the cyberdelic ecosystem.

### Immersive Tech Giants & Hardware
- [Meta Reality Labs](https://about.meta.com/realitylabs/) - The massive R&D division driving the consumer adoption of standalone VR (Quest) and spatial computing.
- [Valve Index & SteamVR](https://www.valvesoftware.com/en/index) - The high-fidelity PCVR ecosystem often preferred by clinical researchers and high-end cyberdelic creators.
- [OpenBCI](https://openbci.com/) - Key technology provider democratizing access to biosensors and brain-computer interfaces.

### Core Software Engines
- [Unity Technologies for VR](https://unity.com/solutions/vr) - The most widely used real-time 3D development platform for creating consumer cyberdelic apps.
- [Unreal Engine (Epic Games)](https://www.unrealengine.com/) - The high-fidelity alternative often used for photorealistic environments and advanced spatial simulations.

### Industry Deep Dives
- [The Metaverse: And How It Will Revolutionize Everything by Matthew Ball](https://www.amazon.com/Metaverse-How-Will-Revolutionize-Everything/dp/1324092033) - Provides crucial context on the economic and technical scale of the infrastructure cyberdelics rely on.
- [State of the VR Industry (UploadVR)](https://uploadvr.com/) - Continually updated journalism on the business and technological realities of immersive tech providers.
